About Prewitt Realty

We are open minded progressive thinkers so everyone is always welcome in our units.

We have had and will continue to have a very diverse "Family Of Tenants" including handicapped, disability, section 8, every race, color, creed, familial status, national origin & sexual orientation.

Being proud that many of our "Long Term" tenants have been with us since we started 34 years ago and everybody is considered uniformly.

We are not a public apartment finding service for tenants but simply a small selfmade company here in "Bridgeport USA".

Thanks for checking out Bill's little web site. Best Rents is a small one man sole proprietorship located in "Bridgeport USA" and he prides himself on making apartments available that are usually under market prices to be competitive.

Bill is a senior citizen who has had a colorful life and should have retired years ago but enjoys the challenges of providing affordable housing for his lucky tenants. He has been in Bridgeport and online for over 30 years and is in it for the "long run".

Some of his tenants have been with him from the very beginning which speaks for itself and there are lots of character references if you take a peek.
